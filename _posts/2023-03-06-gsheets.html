Instructions: To view the data in Google sheets, you must log into a Google account. Once you have done this, click the desired file's link below, and confirm you wish a copy. A google spreadsheet will open in your browser. In order to inspect the data (eg. re-sorting rows by state or year), manipulate it according to your needs (eg. keeping a subset of the observations only), or saving the file to your hard drive, you must first type CTRL+A (ie., select all) then CTRL+SHIFT+V (ie., paste values only) — this will unlink the data from the repository.
