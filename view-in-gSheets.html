The links here open election returns from the https://github.com/emagar/elecRetrns repository in online spreadsheet form. Please report bugs to emagar at itam dot mx. Last-modified: 10mar2023

Basic instructions: a. To use this feature, you must first log into a Google account. Then click the desired file's link below, and confirm you wish a copy. A Google spreadsheet will open in your browser. b. In order to inspect or manipulate the data (eg. re-sorting rows by year or keeping a subset of the observations only), or to save the file to your hard drive, you must unlink the data from the repository. To do this type CTRL+A (ie., select all) then CTRL+SHIFT+V (ie., paste values only). c. Linked data updates about every hour. If a refresh were needed sooner, erase the function in cell A1 and undo the change.
